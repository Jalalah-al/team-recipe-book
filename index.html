jalalah Brownies recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter, melted
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup (40g) cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup (95g) flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8 pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter, sugar, and eggs until smooth.
  3. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, and salt until just combined.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips, then spread batter into pan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Let cool completely before cutting.
